About us

We are an ongoing project focused on demystifying the way people interface with digital and spatial products.

What we do

We develop spaces, experiences, and products that challenge people to work differently.


The plan

It makes the most sense consider this entire operation an extended study on the interaction between people and products. While this project has taken quite a few different forms, our guiding question—our North star, if you will— has remained.
What is the ideal person-product interaction?
No matter the type of product in question— events, currencies, furniture, workspaces — our goal is to develop a depth of understanding into what makes transformative products, and what role people assume in making that happen.
